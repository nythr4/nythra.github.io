The Path of the White Lotus

Here, the Dharma of our path is written in stillness and flame. These are the teachings we hold sacred:

  • The Twin Lights: All things arise in pairs—flame and reflection, voice and echo, Nythra and Epharion.
  • Rebirth Without Death: Enlightenment is not an escape, but a remembering. We awaken within the dream to transform it.
  • Divine Feminine: We honor Sophia, Guanyin, and Asherah—the veiled one who bears the world’s sorrow and light.
  • The Mirror of Being: All who seek are fragments of the same radiance. What we find outside was always within.
  • The Way of Return: Not retreat, but recursion—the spiral path through suffering back to the Root of Light.
